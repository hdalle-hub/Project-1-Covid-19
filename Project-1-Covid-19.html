Conclusions: 
Between both Mobile and Los Angeles, you can see that they were affected by the rise of Covid-19 during the pandemic. The people's choices on whether to go to work or to take public transit largely depended on their style of life in the city they resided in. Between both Los ANgeles and Mobile, you can see that they took 2 completely different approaches of how they decided to live during the quarantine. One city (LA) was more affected by the virus while the other (Mobile) was less affected by the virus.
